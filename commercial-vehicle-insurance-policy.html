Integrated Grievance Management System

IRDA has launched the Integrated Grievance Management System (IGMS). IGMS is a grievance redress monitoring tool for IRDA. Policyholders who have grievances should register their complaints with the Grievance Redress Channel of the Insurance Company first. If policyholders are not able to access the insurance company directly for any reason, IGMS provides a gateway to register complaints with insurance companies. Complaints shall be registered with insurance companies first and only if need be, be escalated them to IRDA (Consumer Affairs Department).
